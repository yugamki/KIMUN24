Welcome to Kumaraguru MUN'24

We are thrilled to host the Chapter 2 of Kumaraguru Model United Nations 2024 (K-MUN 2024) which is a paramount event of the Kumaraguru Institutions. This marks the beginning of a platform that aims to reach out to student ambassadors and leaders from colleges and institutions around the country, K-MUN brings together over 300 enthusiastic and impressionable minds to offer fresh approaches to urgent global issues to provide the aspiring diplomats with an opportunity to engage in constructive dialogue, develop leadership skills, exchange ideas and opinions and foster a deep understanding of global issues. With this edition we vow to take MUNing to a whole new enriching and enjoyable experience for the delegates. Happy MUNing!
